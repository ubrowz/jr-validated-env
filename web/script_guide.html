Design of Experiments

DoE lets you vary multiple process factors simultaneously to find which ones drive your response — with far fewer runs than one-factor-at-a-time testing. Two scripts work together:

Step 1jrc_doe_design — generates the randomised run matrix as a printable lab sheet + companion CSV
Step 2jrc_doe_analyse — reads the completed CSV and produces ANOVA, effect plots, and a significance summary

Use the planner below to choose the right design type, then follow the --help output for each script.
